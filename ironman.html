Iron Man

Stark returned to the United States having changed since his near death experience, striving to be a better person he stopped his company from manufacturing of weapons and put them on the path of inventing new technologies for the betterment of all mankind. He streamlined the suit putting the armour to good use by battling evil doers as the super hero The Invincible Iron-Man. He constantly is redesigning his suit again and again over the years each version slightly better than the last.

Stark has saved the world countless times and is a founding member of the 'The Avengers'.

Why is this character loved so much?

Has no actual superpowers

Despite fighting alongside literal gods and super-soilders Stark has no actual powers, under the armour he is simply a man but that hasn't stopped him fighting hulks, celestials and many others with power beyond comprehension and still coming out on top.

His complex personality

Tony Stark has a massive personality and ego. On the surface he seems arrogant and sarcastic when in actuality he is one of the most caring and thoughtful heroes out there that is incredibly loyal and brave putting his life on the line to save the world a million times over.

Conquered Alcoholism

Many heroes suffer from weakness and internal conflicts but none more than Tony Stark who has to deal with his weakness every second of everyday. While he has quit drinking stark for a long time would use the iron man armour when intoxicated and struggled for a long time with this addiction. But this is not an illness that simply goes away and the iron man comics never ignore this, it is an everyday internal uphill battle to stay on the wagon especially with the pressures he faces as a superhero.

Founding member of the avengers

Together with Ant-Man, Wasp and Hulk, Iron Man helped form the Avengers to defend Earth against threats. He housed the Avengers in a mansion that was their base of operations for many many years and still funds the avengers team, its not cheap paying for all that spandex.

Genius level intellect

One thing that makes Stark so interesting is his intelligence, he invented his armour from a box of scraps in a cave and is constantly upgrading and tinkering with his inventions to prepare for any possible occasion. Tony Stark has foreseen the need for a Hulkbuster, Thorbuster, and all kinds of Marvel hero Busting Armors. He has even invented a sentient armor that has bonded with his skin.
