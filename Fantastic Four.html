Fantastic Four

Reed discovered he could stretch his body to ridiculous lengths being able to convert his entire body into a highly malleable state at will, allowing him to deform, and reform himself into virtually any shape and took up the mantle Mr Fantastic, Sue was able to bend light waves allowing her to render herself and other objects invisible. as well as generate forcefields with her mind, Johnny could now engulf his entire body in flames, is able to fly, can absorb fire harmlessly into his own body, and can control any nearby fire by sheer force of will however all appeared normal when not using their powers, ben was not so lucky, he was transformed into an orange-colored, thick-skinned, heavily muscled, superhumanly strong Thing. Together they were the Fantastic Four, Earth's first family of super heroes.

Why are these character's loved so much?

They are a family

First and foremost, before they are a superhero team they are a family. The Fantastic Four stories are a family of adventurers who occasionally take a break from scientific adventuring to save the world, or the universe on a good day. The family dynamic is a huge part of what makes their comic different, and the scientific adventuring is a good thematic break from the typical superhero story.

Best catchphrases in comics

Johnny storm and ben grimm have two of the best catchphrases in all of comics, Flame on and its clobbering time.

The Fantastic Four Don’t Have To Do This

The best thing about the Fantastic Four is, unlike many other heroes in the marvel universe is that they don’t have a dark event that made them swear vengeance on those who do the world harm. The Fantastic Four have no such driving force simply put, it’s patriotic altruism and an insatiable desire to learn that guides the team. This family chooses to save earth again and again. They just choose to be heroes. That’s just who they are.

Doom

The FF have one of the best if not the best villain in the whole of marvel, victor von doom. Dr Doom is a scarred man incased in an armour that rules over his country of latveria and is the ff’s arch enemy, specifically reeds. Doom has been elevated to the god of the entire mulitverse and thats just a small fraction of his many feats over the years.
